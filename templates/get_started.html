  • Templates

    Django’s template language is designed to strike a balance between power and ease. It’s designed to feel comfortable and easy-to-learn to those used to working with HTML, like designers and front-end developers. But it is also flexible and highly extensible, allowing develoeprs to augment the template language as needed. Read More

        <h1>All Bands</h1>
        <ul>
        {% templatetag openblock %} for band in bands {% templatetag closeblock %}
            <li>
                <h2><a href="{% templatetag openvariable %} band.get_absolute_url {% templatetag closevariable %}">{% templatetag openvariable %} band.name {% templatetag closevariable %}</a></h2>

        </ul>
    </body>
